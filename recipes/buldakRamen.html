Buldak Ramen Recipe

This is a simple Buldak ramen recipe

Very delicious noodles with a little bit of spicy

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • egg
  • teokbokki
  • sauce
  • sesame

Steps

  1. Boil an egg for 7 minutes.
  2. Put the noodles and tteokbokki into boiling water for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the water, remain 5 tablespoons of water.
  4. Add the sauce and stir well.
  5. Add the egg and some sesame seeds.

Carbonara Tteokbokki Recipe

This is a simple carbonara tteokbokki recipe

Very delicious tteokbokki with a bacon and broccoli

Ingredients

  • rice flour
  • water
  • sault
  • milk
  • bacon
  • oil
  • broccoli
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Take 200 gr of rice flour.
  2. Gradually add 100 ml of water into the flour and stir.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of sault.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Cut the dough into equal finger-sized pieces.
  6. Put the pieces into boiling water for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove the water after tteokbokki cooked.
  8. Put a little bit of oil into the pan and add bacon.
  9. Cook the bacon for 4 minutes.
  10. Add tteokbokki and broccoli to the bacon.
  11. Pour the milk into the pan and stir over medium heat.
  12. Add sault if needed.
  13. Cook everything for another 4-10 minutes.
  14. Chop green onion and add to carbonara tteokbokki.

Kimbap Recipe

This is a simple kimbap recipe

Very delicious kimbap with a carrot and an egg

Ingredients

  • short grain rice
  • nori seaweed | kim
  • carrot
  • egg
  • cucumber
  • sausage
  • sault
  • oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice several times in cold water.
  2. Put the rice into boiling water and cook for 10 minutes over high heat.
  3. Add sault.
  4. Cook the rice for another 5 minutes over medium heat.
  5. Remove the water after the rice is cooked.
  6. Mix the white and yolk and pour into a frying pan.
  7. Remove the egg pancake from the frying pan and cut it into thin pieces.
  8. Cut a carrot into thin slices.
  9. Pour oil into the frying pan and add the carrot.
  10. Cut the sausage into thin slices and add to the pan.
  11. Fry everything for 5 minutes.
  12. Cut a cucumber into thin slices.
  13. Place the rice on the nori. leave 2 cm at the top.
  14. Place the chopped egg on the rice.
  15. Place carrot and cucumber on the rice.
  16. Place the sausage on the rice.
  17. Roll the nori with everything into a tube.
  18. Brush the kimbap (nori) with oil.
  19. Cut the kimbap into equal pieces.
