Buldak Ramen Recipe

This is a simple Buldak ramen recipe

Very delicious noodles with a little bit of spicy

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • egg
  • teokbokki
  • sauce
  • sesame

Steps

  1. Boil an egg for 7 minutes.
  2. Put the noodles and tteokbokki into boiling water for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the water, remain 5 tablespoons of water.
  4. Add the sauce and stir well.
  5. Add the egg and some sesame seeds.

Carbonara Tteokbokki Recipe

This is a simple carbonara tteokbokki recipe

Very delicious tteokbokki with a bacon and broccoli

Ingredients

  • rice flour
  • water
  • sault
  • milk
  • bacon
  • oil
  • broccoli
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Take 200 gr of rice flour.
  2. Gradually add 100 ml of water into the flour and stir.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of sault.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Cut the dough into equal finger-sized pieces.
  6. Put the pieces into boiling water for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove the water after tteokbokki cooked.
  8. Put a little bit of oil into the pan and add bacon.
  9. Cook the bacon for 4 minutes.
  10. Add tteokbokki and broccoli to the bacon.
  11. Pour the milk into the pan and stir over medium heat.
  12. Add sault if needed.
  13. Cook everything for another 4-10 minutes.
  14. Chop green onion and add to carbonara tteokbokki.
